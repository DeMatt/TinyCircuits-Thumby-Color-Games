A complete port of the iconic animated music video

Press B to stop the video
Press A to print memory usage (if connected to a computer)
Press RB to toggle framerate display (approximate)

More information available at https://github.com/transistortester/thumby-bad-apple as well as in the description of https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cyVtrq1OPQo
