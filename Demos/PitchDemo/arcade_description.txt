Pitch Demo provides a simple tone-playing utility for testing the Thumby Color.

Features:
- Two independent tones which can be toggled on and off.
- Preset tone adjustment (A440 base, 12 Equal Temperament intervals).
- Frequency AND letter/octave identification.
- Frequencies are limited:
    - Minimum:  110 Hz, A2
    - Maximum:  7040 Hz, A8

Controls:
- Left/Right: adjust tone A's frequency up/down a semitone.
- LB/RB: adjust tone B's frequency up/down a semitone.
- A: toggle tone A on or off.
- B: toggle tone B on or off.
- Menu: exit.
